OVERVIEW

The BlockChanger is a Network Intrusion Detections/Prevention system that will provide the blockchain authentication approach to all IoT network devices once they are connected to it. The value that BlockChanger adds is its agentless functionality. In other words, it does not require any software or agent to be installed inside the IoT devices to be part of the blockchain network.
